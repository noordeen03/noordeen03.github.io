Rules and Regulations

1) All content must be yours, or you should have right to use and license it. No copyright-infringing materials will be permitted.
2) Prototypes are not required. However, if a working prototype does exist, please indicate. Contestants are encouraged to include an illustration of their prototype with the entry materials.
3) Grand Prize winners will be determined by the Judges. All decisions by the Judges are final.
4) Winners will be notified by phone and email announced in March 30, 2017

Winners / Prizes

1) Winners will be announced on March 23, 2018. There will be (3) winners. The Grand Prize winner will receive a Cash award of Rs 3000.
